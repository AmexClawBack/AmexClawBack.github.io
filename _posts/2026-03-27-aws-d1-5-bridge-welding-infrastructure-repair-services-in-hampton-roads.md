## **What is AWS D1.5 and Why It Matters**

AWS D1.5 is the governing code for bridge welding, specifically developed to ensure the structural integrity and safety of steel bridges and infrastructure. Unlike standard structural welding, bridge welding involves:

- Higher inspection standards
- Strict welding procedures and qualifications
- Critical load-bearing connections
- Work performed in challenging field conditions

Choosing a contractor experienced in AWS D1.5 welding ensures that all work meets DOT specifications and passes inspection without delays.
