Billboard structures are classified as **structural steel**, meaning the welding involved is far beyond cosmetic or light fabrication. These signs are exposed to:

- Public safety risk if a failure occurs

Because of this, billboard welding often involves:

- Structural fillet and groove welds
- Field splices on columns and catwalks
- Bracing, ladders, handrails, and maintenance platforms
- Repairs to existing structures after storm or vehicle damage
