## Why AWS D1.5 Certification Matters for Bridge Welding

The **AWS D1.5 Bridge Welding Code** is the governing standard for welding on bridges and critical infrastructure. It outlines strict requirements for:

- Welding procedures and qualifications
- Inspection and testing protocols
- Structural integrity of carbon and low-alloy steel bridges
- Compliance with AASHTO and federal transportation standards

Unlike standard structural welding (AWS D1.1), **AWS D1.5 is significantly more stringent**, requiring tighter control over heat input, welding processes, and inspection criteria to ensure long-term durability and safety.
