## What Is RTU Curb Welding?

An RTU curb is a raised structural frame mounted on a roof to support HVAC rooftop units. The curb must be securely welded to steel roof decking, structural beams, or support frames.

RTU curb welding typically involves:

- Structural steel welding to roof framing
- Welding curb frames to steel supports
- Reinforcing connections and brackets
- Sealing welds to prevent water intrusion
- Compliance with AWS D1.1 structural welding standards

Because RTU curbs directly impact structural integrity and weatherproofing, welding must be performed by qualified professionals‚Äînot general labor or HVAC installers alone.
